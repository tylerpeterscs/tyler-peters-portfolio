The Protagonist

Hello! I am Tyler.

I'm a Computer Science major based in my hometown Salt Lake City, Utah and set to finish my CS credits Fall 2025.

I will finish my minor in Business to graduate Spring 2026.

I'm a passionate and curious computer science student with a strong interest in UI/UX design and full-stack development. I enjoy crafting intuitive, accessible interfaces that prioritize user readability and experience—ensuring that applications are easy to navigate for everyone. Full-stack development excites me because it blends creative problem-solving with technical depth; I love designing sleek frontends while building robust, scalable backends. I find fulfillment in creating software that not only works well but feels great to use.

In my free time, I enjoy reading literature, especially fantasy novels, my current all time favorite is A Song of Ice and Fire (also known as Game of Thrones) by George R. R. Martin. I also enjoy spending my time outdoors whether that be camping, skiing, or mountain biking.
